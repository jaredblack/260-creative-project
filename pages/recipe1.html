Grilled Cheese Sandwich

5 stars

15 min

4-6 servings

Ingredients
  • 12 slices bread (preferably sourdough)
  • 6 oz cheese of your choice (cheddar, gruyere, gouda, swiss)
  • Butter or mayonnaise
  • 8 oz tomato soup (optional)
Instructions
  1. Heat up a griddle or large frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Melt butter in frying pan.
    • If using mayonnaise, coat both sides of bread with mayonnaise instead.
  3. Place one side of each slice of bread on the pan, thoroughly coating each slice with butter. Leave one slice of bread in the pan, and take the other out.
  4. Place cheese across full surface of the piece of bread still in the pan, then replace the other slice, buttered side facing outward.
  5. Place lid over pan to ensure cheese melts before bread burns. If you don't have a lid, reduce heat so that the cheese melts before the bread burns.
  6. When sandwich is golden-brown on one side, flip to the other side. Remove from heat when that side is golden-brown. Serve immediately, with tomato soup, if that's your thing.
